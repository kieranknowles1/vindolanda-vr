Vindolandria VR

360 views of the fort today, with a button to see what it might have looked like. As historically accurate as possible. An educational purpose.

Dual rendering for transition between present day and Rome. Need to be mindful about performance and motion sickness. Could use Portal Reloaded time portals as inspiration.

Have 3 months be careful about scope creep.

Maybe do “a day in the life”, and go into further detail about one or two areas.

Performance is essential. Most headsets require 90-120fps for two viewports. Drivers add reprojection automatically which should only be used as a fallback.
